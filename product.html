Our Product Range

Premium Cocopeat & Coir Products for Global Growers

Cocopeat Blocks

Available in 5kg and 650g briquettes. Washed & low EC cocopeat suitable for vegetables, flowers, and hydroponics. Expands 70-75 liters (5kg block).

Coir Discs

Pressed coir discs ideal for seed raising, nurseries, and young plant propagation. Available in multiple diameters (10cm–30cm).

Grow Bags

Ready-to-use grow bags filled with washed & buffered cocopeat and husk chip blends. Widely used for tomatoes, cucumbers, strawberries, and peppers.

Husk Chips

Coir husk chips improve aeration and drainage in growing media. Available in graded sizes for potting mixes and orchid substrates.

Potting Mix & Custom Blends

Tailor-made mixes of cocopeat, husk chips, perlite, and nutrients prepared to buyer specifications for horticulture and hydroponics.

Cocochip Cake

Size 30 × 30 × 12 cm (approx.), 30 × 30 × 15 cm (for higher compression / bigger cakes). Weight 4.5 – 5 kg (standard export size). Expansion on rehydration yields ~60–70 liters of husk chips when soaked in water.
